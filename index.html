How to use
1. Enter Players either one by one or via player list.

2. Click on 'Team Generator' then on 'Generate Teams'. (Allow a few seconds for the program to load!)

3. Enjoy your generated matchup!

Player Lists
1. One player per line.

2. Do not include any spaces in the player name.

3. Ranks must be abbreviated. Do not include LP. (Bronze 1 = B1, Plat 2 = P2, Grandmaster = GM, Challenger = C)

4. Main Role and off role must also be abbreviated.

5. Example Formatting:
Player1 G3 TOP MID
Player2 S2 JNG ADC
